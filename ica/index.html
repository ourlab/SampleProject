ICA in a Nutshell

Innovation Center Ajman (ICA) is located in Ajman University of Science & Technology (AUST), United Arab Emirates (UAE). The purpose of establishing this Center is to promote innovation and entrepreneurship in the UAE through effectively engaging all the relevant stakeholders to address the industrial issues, market requirements, government expectations and societal needs. ICA aims at playing a significant role in the rapid and sustainable growth of the national economy to help achieve the UAE’s “Vision 2021”.
